FAQ

What stage are these apps at?

Early. They work, we use them daily, and we are still actively shaping them. Expect things to move, get renamed, and occasionally break while we figure out what each app should really be.

Will my data stick around?

Your data is yours. We will not sell it or hand it off to advertisers. While the apps are free and under evaluation we cannot guarantee 100 percent uptime or that no migration will ever go sideways. Keep your own copies of anything you cannot afford to lose. We will do our best, and we will always give you a way to export what you put in.

Is it going to stay free?

We intend to grow factor317 into a viable commercial product. Even after that, there will always be a free version of each app, at minimum letting you read and export your own data. You will not be locked out of your own work.

It is slow / something is broken. What do I do?

Tell us. Performance will be uneven in the early days, and we would rather hear about it than not. Email info@factor317.com. If you ask nicely we will do our best, often quickly.

Can I request a feature?

Yes. We take feedback seriously. We cannot promise everything will get built, but ideas from real users are how these apps get better.

How do I support the work?

The apps are free. If you want to help us keep building, you can drop a tip at Buy Me a Coffee. Donations are appreciated, never required, and do not buy you anything other than our gratitude and continued effort.

What do you actually promise?

Honestly: nothing ironclad. We guarantee nothing, promise nothing, but we try hard, we are good people, and we want you to use these apps to the fullest. Delivering real value is what we are after. If we can make a living from the work, that is the cherry on top.
