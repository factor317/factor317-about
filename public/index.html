factor317 Architectural excellence for personal productivity.

An engineering collective

We build for the individual, at scale.

factor317 is an engineering collective focused on the utilitarian application space. We believe software does not need to change the world to be meaningful. It just needs to work exceptionally well for the person using it. By applying nearly two decades of high-scale systems experience to personal-focus tools, we create a digital ecosystem that is modular, iterative, and above all, pragmatic.

01 / Ethos

Anti-hype, by design.

We are not here to disrupt anything. factor317 is a workshop for seasoned software, built by engineers who have spent years navigating large-scale system complexity and now apply that same rigor to small, everyday frictions.

The work is narrow and high-leverage. The vocabulary is robust, iterative, utilitarian, vetted. The promise is modest: we may help you improve a part of your world.

02 / Approach

Small apps, built to stand on their own.

Each factor317 app is scoped to do one thing well. Where it makes sense, apps share common building blocks behind the scenes, but every app is usable on its own terms.

03 / Published

05 / Engineering

Human architecture, agentic execution.

factor317 is a testbed for the future of software development, where human architectural oversight meets agent-driven engineering workflows. It is how a small group sustains a thick pipeline without manufacturing a large team.

The trade-offs that normally get cut from "small" apps, such as injection protection, robust logging, sensible auth boundaries, and observable failure modes, are baked in from the first commit.
